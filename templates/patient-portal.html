Accessing Your Portal

Existing Users

Log In to Patient Portal

First Time Users / Activation

  1. Receive Invitation: You will receive an email invitation from our office (check your inbox and spam folder) to activate your portal account. If you haven't received one, please call us.
  2. Activate Account: Click the "Get Started" link within the invitation email. You'll be guided to create a secure password and verify your identity (usually using your date of birth).
  3. Complete Setup: Follow the on-screen instructions to finish creating your account.
  4. Log In: Once activated, you can log in anytime using the button above or by visiting https://portal.kareo.com.

Need Assistance?

If you haven't received an invitation email, are having trouble activating your account, or can't log in, please call our office during business hours at (423) 373-5457, and we'll be happy to help.

What Is Functional Medicine?

Conventional medicine is excellent at treating acute illness and managing symptoms. Functional medicine goes further — it asks why symptoms are happening in the first place. Using comprehensive labs, detailed health history, and an evidence-based systems approach, we identify the underlying drivers of chronic illness and build a personalized plan to address them.

  • Treats the whole person, not just isolated symptoms
  • Uses advanced testing beyond standard panels
  • Combines nutrition, lifestyle, and targeted supplementation
  • Partners with you — you're an active participant in your care
  • Long appointments: time to actually listen

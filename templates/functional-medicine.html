What is Functional Medicine?

Functional Medicine is a systems-biology-based approach focusing on the unique genetic, biochemical, and lifestyle factors of each individual. By understanding the root causes of complex health issues, we can create personalized strategies to optimize health, prevent disease, and enhance your overall quality of life.

Advanced Diagnostic Testing

We utilize state-of-the-art lab testing to gain precise insights into your unique physiology. Tests may include:

  • GI-MAP: Comprehensive stool analysis for gut health.
  • DUTCH Complete: Advanced hormone testing (urine).
  • Organic Acids Test (OAT): Assessment of metabolic pathways.
  • DUTCH Sex Hormone Metabolites: Detailed sex hormone evaluation.
  • Saliva Cortisol Profile: Adrenal function assessment.
  • Micronutrient Testing (SpectraCell): Identifying vitamin/mineral deficiencies.
  • Toxic & Nutrient Elements: Heavy metal and essential mineral analysis.
  • Hair Elements Analysis: Mineral balance and chronic exposure insights.
  • 3x4 Genetics Test + Blueprint Report: Personalized genetic wellness strategies.
